Kudu's Wet Food

Kudu is given Felix wet cat food. His favourite flavour is Tuna, while his least favourite flavour is Chicken and Tomato.

Kudu's Dry Food

Kudu is given Go Cat on a daily basis.
Ingredients: Cereals, Meat and animal derivatives (20%, of which 3% chicken and 1% duck), Vegetable protein extracts, Oils and fats, Derivatives of vegetable origin, minerals, yeasts
Analytical Constituents: Protein: 30%; Crude Fibres: 3%; Crude Oils and Fats: 11%; Crude Ash: 8%; Moisture: Not Stated; Omega (3 and/or 6): Not Stated; Calcium: Not Stated; Phosphorus: Not Stated

Kudu used to be given Whiskas dry food, but his food specialists determined that it was detrimental to his health in the long term.
